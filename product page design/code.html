The Story

Harvested at high altitudes during the darkest hours of the lunar cycle, these beans are roasted until they reach a deep, obsidian sheen. The process requires precision to capture the intense smokiness without bitterness.

The Flavor

A complex profile featuring heavy notes of dark cocoa, charred cedar, and a lingering molasses sweetness. Perfect for those who enjoy a full-bodied cup that commands attention.

The Roast

We use a dual-stage roasting method to ensure heat penetrates to the core of the bean, developing the oils that provide that signature silky mouthfeel and long finish.
